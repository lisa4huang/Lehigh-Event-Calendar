Take Back Your Body Yoga

Organization: Survivor Support and Intimacy Education

Location: Maida Fitness Studio C230

Start: 2025-09-02T20:30:00+00:00

End: 2025-09-02T21:30:00+00:00

Description:

Trauma-informed yoga is a research proven way to help survivors of sexual trauma and those experiencing vicarious trauma heal from their trauma symptoms. It is a combination of focused breathing and mindfulness skills, as well as healing poses. Trauma-informed yoga is an opportunity to learn how to work with your own discomforts. It is a judgment free zone where every participant is able to go at their own pace and skill level. The practice is not meant to be a high cardio or strength training workout, it focuses on grounding, breathing, and mindfulness with comfortable, stretching poses.

Our instructor, Liz Jordon, is certified in trauma-informed practice. The instructor does not leave her own mat and will never touch participants to adjust their pose throughout the practice. It is a space where your body is entirely yours.

Students, faculty, and staff of all gender identities at all levels of healing from primary, secondary, and vicarious trauma are welcome and can benefit from trauma-informed yoga.

Fall 2025 classes will take place on Tuesday's.

Location: Maida Fitness Studio C230

Time: 4:30 - 5:30pm

Lehigh University 2025 Fall Career Expo

Organization: Center for Career & Professional Development

Location: Stabler Arena

Start: 2025-09-18T19:00:00+00:00

End: 2025-09-18T23:00:00+00:00

Description:

Join us at the Fall 2025 Career Expo, Lehigh's premier recruiting event of the year! Open to all sophomores, juniors, seniors, graduate students, and alumni up to two years post-graduation, this is your chance to connect with top employers, explore internships, co-ops and full-time roles, and take the next step toward your future.

Date: Thursday, September 18, 2025
Time: 3:00 PM - 7:00 PM
Location: Stabler Arena (Goodman Campus)

How to Register & Prepare:

  1. Student registration opens on Monday, August 11th at 8:00 a.m. on Handshake. Click here to add a Google Calendar reminder.
  2. After you register, explore the list of employers attending and review the career expo prep guide.
  3. Periodically check the Career Expo website for updated information about newly added employers and opportunities.

Questions?

Please visit Career Lab drop-in hours at the Center for Career & Professional Development (Monday-Friday, 11-4, Maginnes 500) or email careercenter@lehigh.edu.
